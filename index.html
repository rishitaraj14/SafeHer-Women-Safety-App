Safety Resources

Knowledge is power — essential safety tips and legal rights for every woman

⚖️

Know Your Rights

  • IPC Section 354 – Assault on women
  • IPC Section 376 – Rape & sexual assault
  • POCSO Act – Child protection
  • Domestic Violence Act 2005
  • Sexual Harassment at Workplace Act
🛡️

Safety Tips

  • Share live location with trusted contacts
  • Avoid isolated areas at night
  • Keep emergency numbers saved offline
  • Trust your instincts — leave if unsafe
  • Take self-defence classes if possible
📱

Digital Safety

  • Review app permissions regularly
  • Use strong, unique passwords
  • Avoid sharing location on social media
  • Report cyber crime at cybercrime.gov.in
  • Block & document harassment online
🏃‍♀️

Self-Defence Basics

  • Palm strike to the nose — disorienting
  • Knee to the groin — effective escape
  • Shout "FIRE!" — attracts more attention
  • Carry pepper spray (legal in India)
  • Target eyes, nose, throat, groin
